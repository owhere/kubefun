About Kubefun

Kubefun is an intuitive web-based Kubernetes explorer designed to help you navigate and understand your cluster's resources and their relationships. With Kubefun, you can easily search for any keyword and uncover related resources in your cluster, providing a comprehensive view of your Kubernetes environment.

Why Kubefun?

  • Efficient Search: Use the global search feature to find all resources linked to a keyword, such as namespaces, deployments, pods, services, cluster roles, secrets, and more.
  • Resource Insights: Dive deep into detailed views of resources like Pods, Deployments, Services, Secrets, StorageClasses, and CRDs.
  • Cluster Overview: Get a high-level summary of your cluster, including node health, resource counts, and namespace-level details.
  • Interactivity: Navigate through resources with hyperlinks for related resources, making exploration seamless and efficient.
  • User-Friendly: Designed with simplicity and functionality in mind, Kubefun is accessible for both Kubernetes newcomers and experts.
